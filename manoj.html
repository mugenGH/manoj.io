What is the full form of FMCG FMCG: Fast-Moving Consumer Goods

What is the full form of FMCG FMCG: Fast-Moving Consumer Goods FMCG stands for Fast-Moving Consumer Goods which are also known as consumer packed goods (CPD). These goods refer to the products that are sold quickly and generally non-durable. It is also known as Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG). The examples of FMCG products are soft drinks, processed foods, cosmetics like lip balm, eyeliner, deodorants, soaps, general using products like toilet paper, face towel, shower cap, toilet soaps and the over-the-counter drugs etc.
